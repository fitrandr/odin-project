Pesto Pasta

Description

This dish comes together in 10 minutes and relies on the flavor of the pesto to do the heavy lifting. It is a complete meal that requires only boiling water and mixing

Ingredients

  • 8 oz (250g) pasta of your choice (penne, fusilli, or spaghetti)
  • 1/2 cup jarred pesto (basil or sun-dried tomato)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Optional: Grated Parmesan cheese

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Boil a pot of salted water and cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente.
  2. Before draining, reserve about 2 tablespoons of the starchy pasta water.
  3. Drain the pasta and return it to the hot pot.
  4. Stir in the pesto and the reserved pasta water to create a creamy sauce that coats the noodles.
  5. Fold in the raw cherry tomatoes (the heat of the pasta will slightly warm them) and serve topped with Parmesan
