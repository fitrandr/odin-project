Cheese & Egg Quesadilla

Description

This is arguably the fastest hot meal you can make, ready in just 5 minutes with only 2 main ingredients. It requires no special cooking skills, just a pan and a spatula.

Ingredients

  • 2 flour tortillas
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, or a Mexican blend)
  • Optional: Salsa, sour cream, or leftover cooked chicken

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Place one tortilla in a dry skillet over medium heat.
  2. Sprinkle the cheese evenly over the tortilla, covering the surface.
  3. Top with the second tortilla and cook for 2-3 minutes until the bottom is golden brown and the cheese starts to melt.
  4. Flip carefully and cook the other side for another 2 minutes until crispy.
  5. Remove from heat, slice into wedges, and serve immediately with salsa if desired.
