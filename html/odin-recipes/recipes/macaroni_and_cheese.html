Microwave Mug Mac and Cheese

Description

Ideal for a single serving with zero cleanup, this recipe is ready in 5 minutes using only a microwave-safe mug. It satisfies comfort food cravings without boiling a large pot of water.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup elbow macaroni
  • 1/2 cup water (plus a little more if needed)
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Combine the dry macaroni and water in a large microwave-safe mug or bowl.
  2. Microwave on high for 2 minutes, stir, and then microwave for another 1-2 minutes until the water is mostly absorbed and pasta is cooked (watch closely to prevent boiling over).
  3. Stir in the milk and shredded cheese.
  4. Microwave for another 30-45 seconds until the cheese is fully melted and bubbly.
  5. Stir well to create a creamy sauce, season with salt and pepper, and enjoy directly from the mug
